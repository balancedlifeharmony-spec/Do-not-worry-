About the Author

Emhemed Elahresh is a writer, creator, and founder of Victory Hour Multimedia Inc.—a movement devoted to empowering minds and building meaningful creations that feed both the spirit and the world. Through stories, philosophy, and design, his work blends ancient wisdom with modern awareness to help readers reclaim their inner balance and sense of purpose.

His signature message—“10% to feed the hungry”—runs through every project he creates, turning creativity into a force for good.

Book Summary

The Weight of the World is a book about liberation—not from responsibility, but from illusion. It teaches how to distinguish between what we can control and what we cannot, between caring and worrying, and between presence and performance.

Across twenty chapters, it guides readers through the architecture of attention, the discipline of focus, and the peace that comes from rooting ourselves in what is truly ours to carry. It’s a framework for clarity in an age of noise, a path back to agency, and a reminder that real change begins within.

Fighting World Hunger – The Victory Hour Mission

Hunger is not a lack of food—it’s a lack of access, opportunity, and awareness. Every creation under Victory Hour Multimedia Inc. carries a mission to change that.

10% of all proceeds from this book and every project under the Victory Hour brand go toward food programs, local kitchens, and community networks fighting hunger across the world.

When you buy, read, or share this book—you are part of that mission.

You are feeding hope. You are feeding life. You are Victory Hour.
